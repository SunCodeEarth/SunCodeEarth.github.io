Shipeng Sun

Research Interests

My research interests are primarily in three related areas:

  • GIS Algorithms, geovisualization, sociospatial network analysis, and computational geography;
  • Coupled human-environment systems, agent-based complexity modeling, and environmental geography;
  • Intraurban migration, land and housing markets, land use change, and urban geography.
